The power of TikTok

TikTok is no longer the new kid on the block. With a respected ad platform and its innovative TikTok Shop feature, it is one of the fastest growing social media platforms. Increasingly, brands are turning to TikTok to drive conversions at scale, creating real brand impact.

In 2022, 1/3 of marketers wanted to start using TikTok, while 45% were already using the platform for influencer marketing. And with 50% of users saying TikTok helps them decide what to buy, it’s no surprise brands are looking to adopt it as a core part of the marketing mix. 

There are no two ways about it: the platform has exploded in the 2020s and TikTok optimisation is finding itself at the heart of communications strategies. 

But it takes time, effort and resources to get it right. From organic content to brand awareness through TikTok ads, Smoking Gun is the social media agency that can support you now.

Watch our ‘Brand Building on TikTok’ webinar here for 5 essential insights.

Who’s on TikTok?

In the early days, TikTok was seen as a platform solely for Gen Z. But not anymore.

The channel has exploded in recent years, and the TikTok influencer is now king. The number of UK TikTok users rose from 9.8 million in 2020 to over 13.3 million in 2021, and app downloads are set to reach 16.8 million by 2024. 

While the younger generation is always the first to use new technologies, just as they did with Facebook and Instagram, the average member age is rising quickly, with a huge demographic and wide audience. As Millennials go beyond platforms like Snapchat or even LinkedIn, we’re seeing new opportunities every day.

With millions of users on TikTok daily, there are great case studies for investing in this platform. We’ve witnessed many different age groups are constantly creating, communicating, learning, shopping and using the app for their own entertainment. This presents a huge opportunity for brands. If your brand is looking for a TikTok advertising agency, you’ve come to the right place.
